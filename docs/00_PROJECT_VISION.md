# Round AI Project Vision

## Purpose

Round AI is a web-based clinical data capture system for hospitals that still rely on paper records.

The first objective is not to build a full EMR, hospital management system, AI doctor, or decision-support platform.

The first objective is to capture paper-based clinical documents reliably, label them correctly, transcribe or OCR them when useful, and gradually convert them into high-quality structured clinical data.

## Core Product Sentence

Round AI converts paper-based ward documents into searchable, research-ready clinical data.

## Primary Users in Alpha

- Doctor-founder/developer collecting data personally
- Later: research assistants
- Later: doctors and ward staff
- Later: hospital research teams

## Alpha Principle

The alpha must be boring, fast, and reliable.

It should answer one question:

Can one person use a phone browser during or after ward rounds to create a patient record, create an encounter, add clinical documents, upload pages, and enter usable text without frustration?

## Product Boundary

Round AI is not initially:

- A full electronic medical record
- A billing system
- An appointment system
- A prescribing tool
- A clinical decision support system
- An AI diagnosis system
- A hospital-wide deployment

## Long-Term Direction

Round AI may later support:

- OCR correction workflows
- AI document classification
- AI extraction of medicines, diagnoses, lab values, and outcomes
- Dynamic research fields
- Cohort search
- Research exports
- Audit dashboards
- Clinical AI dataset preparation

But all of that depends on first building a clean capture pipeline.
